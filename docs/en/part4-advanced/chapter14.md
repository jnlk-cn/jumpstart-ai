# Chapter 14: Business Thinking

## What You'll Learn

- From requirements to implementation: bridging the gap
- Understanding business value of AI features
- Communicating with stakeholders who don't understand AI
- Measuring success: metrics that matter
- Prioritizing features when resources are limited

Technical skills alone won't make you a great AI developer. You need to understand the business context, speak the language of product managers and executives, and deliver solutions that create real value.
