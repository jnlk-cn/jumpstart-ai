# Chapter 8: From Prompt Engineering to Agent Development

## What You'll Learn

Before starting the formal content, let me ask you a question: Suppose you entered the AI development field in March 2023. What was hottest back then? It was Prompt Engineers—a job that sounds cool but is essentially just "knows how to write prompts." Back then, AI application development was simple to the point of being absurd: you write a Prompt, AI returns an answer, done.

But if you try to make a living with just "writing prompts" in 2026, you'll likely starve.

The core task of this chapter is to help you **build a global perspective**. You will clearly see:

1. **Why Prompt Engineering is just the starting point**: What it can do, where its ceiling is
2. **How Chains solved these ceilings**: What the earliest engineering practices looked like
3. **Why Agents are the inevitable evolution**: From "I tell you what to do" to "you decide what to do"
4. **What Agent Assistants mean**: The qualitative change when adding memory, personality, and long-term goals
5. **Where you should stand now**: The 2026 ecosystem map and selection decisions

**Prerequisites**: Chapter 5 Prompt Engineering Basics, Chapter 7 AI Agent Core Logic.
